Breakfast


Oatmeal pancakes with banana

Kids love pancakes. In addition, it is a very fun recipe to make with them, and to prepare them for breakfast. To make it a healthy breakfast for children, you can go to cereals such as oatmeal, less sugar and more fruit.

Time: 20 minutes.

Ingredients for 4 people.

  • 5 tablespoons oat flour
  • 100 grams of strawberries
  • 75 ml of vegetable milk
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • ½ teaspoon yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • 1 egg
  • 2 bananas
  • Butter
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • Salt

Elaboration.

  • Step 1. Crack the egg into a bowl and beat it together with the sugar. Add the milk and mix.
  • Step 2. Sift over the flour with the yeast, cinnamon and a pinch of salt.
  • Step 3. Peel the plantains. Crush one of them with a fork and add it to the bowl. Flavor with vanilla. Beat until you get a homogeneous cream.
  • Step 4. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a small nonstick skillet and curdle the previous dough into pancake shapes. Cook for 2 minutes on each side. Remove them and reserve them.
  • Step 5. Clean the strawberries, wash them and cut them with the remaining banana into slices.
  • Step 6. Distribute the pancakes on the plates interspersing pancakes and layers of fruit. Pour the honey on top and serve.
  • The trick: To prevent the bananas from darkening in contact with the air, sprinkle them with lemon juice.

Lunch


Asparagus in sauce

Asparagus from Navarra, with designation of origin, stands out for its great flavor. They are diuretic, cleansing and help stimulate kidney function.

Time: 25 minutes.

Ingredients for 4 people.

  • 12-16 asparagus from Navarra (very thick)
  • 100 g of smoked salmon
  • 1 onion
  • 30ml lemon juice
  • 100 ml of cooking cream
  • A few sprigs of thyme
  • A handful of sprouts or sprouts
  • 4 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Elaboration.

  • Step 1. Peel the onion and chop finely. In a skillet, heat two tablespoons of oil. Add the onion and sauté over low heat for 10-15 minutes, or until it begins to be tender. While it is being done, chop the smoked salmon into strips.
  • Step 2. When the onion is fried, add the liquid cream and a few drops of lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper and cook for a couple of minutes, until the mixture begins to thicken.
  • Step 3. Turn off the heat and add the bits of salmon, lemon and some previously chopped thyme leaves. Then reserve the sauce.
  • Step 4. Drain the asparagus carefully. In a large nonstick skillet, heat the rest of the oil. Add the asparagus and brown them for one minute, over high heat. Turn them over. They don't need to be completely browned, just warming them up will suffice.
  • Step 5. Arrange the asparagus on plates and drizzle with the prepared sauce. Garnish with the sprouts and serve.

Desert


Light tangerine mousse

Although to prepare this dessert you will need to let it rest for a while, it is so tasty and it will take so little to make it that you will not be able to resist.

Time: 15 minutes.

Ingredients for 4 people.

  • 250 ml of mandarin juice
  • 1 envelope of neutral gelatin
  • 1 natural skimmed yogurt
  • 1 tangerine
  • A few mint leaves

Elaboration.

  • Step 1. Bring 125 ml of water to a boil. Remove it from the heat and add the gelatin and half the juice. Stir the mixture, transfer it to a bowl and let it rest for a while.
  • Step 2. Watch out! Look when the previous preparation begins to thicken slightly, and before it has set, beat it with the rods until it doubles its volume, gradually incorporating the rest of the juice mixed with the yogurt.
  • Step 3. Divide the mousse into 4 glasses and let it rest in the fridge for 3 or 4 hours, until it is curdled.
  • Step 4. Peel the tangerine, separate the segments and remove the skins. Wash some mint leaves and dry them. Decorate the mousses with the tangerine segments and the mint leaves, and serve them.
  • The trick: although the tangerine juice is quite sweet, if you wish you can add a teaspoon of honey or sugar.

Dinner


Salad

If you're not a big fan of lettuce salads, try this recipe. A warm salad with vegetables, and very quick to make. The dressing with feta cheese will give it the tastiest Mediterranean touch.

Time: 50 minutes.

Ingredients for 4 people.

  • ¼ red cabbage
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 red onion
  • 100g rocket
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 garlic
  • feta cheese
  • 100g hazelnuts
  • Some onion sprouts
  • Capers
  • Parsley
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Oil and salt

Elaboration.

  • Step 1. Peel the onion and cut it off and wash the zucchini. Cut both into sheets and roast them in the oven for about three minutes at 200º.
  • Step 2. Remove the onion and zucchini from the oven, and let cool. Peel the garlic, drain the capers and wash some parsley leaves; beat them with 50 ml of vinegar and 100 ml of oil until emulsified.
  • Step 3. Pour the sauce into a bowl and let the roasted vegetables marinate in it. Meanwhile, scrape the carrots, wash them and cut them into very thin strips with a mandolin.
  • Step 4. Peel the avocados and cut them in half; remove the bone and cut the pulp into slices.
  • Step 5. Clean the cabbage and cut it. Wash the sprouts and arugula and dry them.
  • Step 6. Distribute the red cabbage, the carrot, the avocado and the drained marinated vegetables on the plates. Add the feta cheese, the sprouts, the arugula and the chopped hazelnuts. Season with the sauce and serve.
